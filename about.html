Sunset Festival 2021

The first edition of Sunset Festival took place in the summer of 2011 with the idea to promote culture for the whole family and get the music scene closer to the people. Since then, every year, the festival gathers top of the notch artist and bands of the local scene to offer a full day of music and entertainment of the highest level and security.
